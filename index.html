Repairs & Services

Road • Gravel • Mountain • Commuter • Kids

  • Flat fixes & tires: tubes, tubeless setup, tire swaps, sealant refresh
  • Wheels: bearing service, hub/freehub inspection, spoke replacement, minor truing
  • Brakes: pad replacement, rotor alignment, cable/housing, lever adjustment, hydraulic bleed
  • Drivetrain: chain & cassette replacement, derailleur tune, hanger alignment, shifting diagnostics
  • Cockpit & fit basics: bar tape, grips, saddle/seatpost, lever position
  • Bottom bracket & cranks: creak tracking, torque checks, replacement (cartridge/press‑fit)
  • Suspension (MTB): sag & rebound setup, seal checks, hardware torque, basic service
  • General tune‑ups: safety check, bolt torque, cleaning, lubrication, ride‑ready adjustments
  • Assembly & checks: new‑bike builds, cargo/child‑seat installs, accessory mounting (racks, lights, fenders)
